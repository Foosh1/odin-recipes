Pancake Recipe

Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

This is a pancake recipe that some random dude on the internet posted.

He found it in his grandmothers recipe book so I bet they are pretty

tasty. Now some random guy doing The Odin Project is going to

incorporate this recipe into his first project. Stay tuned for

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
